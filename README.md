# Business Problem

A telecom company was experiencing customer churn but lacked visibility into which customers were leaving, when they were leaving, and the revenue impact of churn.

# What I Did

* Cleaned and validated 7,043 customer records using PostgreSQL.
* Analyzed churn by contract type, tenure, and customer value.
* Built a Power BI dashboard to identify churn drivers and quantify revenue risk.
* Modeled the financial impact of reducing churn among high risk customer segments.

# Key Findings

![Dashboard](https://github.com/Anthonia-Awoyemi-Analytics/telecom-churn-retention-analysis/raw/main/Telechurn.png)

* Overall churn rate: 26.54%.
* 89% of churn came from month to month customers.
* Customers within their first 6 months showed the highest churn risk.
* Churned customers paid higher monthly charges than retained customers.
* Reducing month to month churn by 10% could retain 166 customers and protect approximately $145K in annual revenue.

# Recommendation

Prioritize onboarding, engagement, and contract conversion strategies for early stage month to month customers to reduce revenue loss and improve retention.
