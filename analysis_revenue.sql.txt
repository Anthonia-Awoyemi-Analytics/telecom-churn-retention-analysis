06_analysis_revenue.sql
Purpose:
Revenue-focused analysis for the cleaned telecom customer dataset.
This file compares revenue characteristics of churned and retained customers, then estimates the financial upside of a retention improvement scenario.

Source table:
customer_churn_clean

A) Revenue comparison by churn status compares customer counts, average monthly charges, and average total charges between churned and retained customers
SELECT
  churn,
  COUNT(*) AS total_customers,
  ROUND(AVG(monthly_charges), 2) AS avg_monthly_charges,
  ROUND(AVG(total_charges), 2) AS avg_total_charges
FROM customer_churn_clean
GROUP BY churn;

B) Month to month revenue comparison
   Compares average monthly charges for churners vs retained customers within the highest risk contract segment
SELECT
  contract,
  churn,
  ROUND(AVG(monthly_charges), 2) AS avg_monthly_charges
FROM customer_churn_clean
WHERE contract = 'Month-to-month'
GROUP BY contract, churn;

C) Retention improvement scenario
   Models the estimated monthly and annual revenue protected, if Month to month churn is reduced by 10%
SELECT
  COUNT(*) AS churned_customers,
  ROUND(AVG(monthly_charges), 2) AS avg_monthly_charge,
  ROUND(COUNT(*) * 0.10, 0) AS customers_saved_10pct,
  ROUND(COUNT(*) * 0.10 * AVG(monthly_charges), 2) AS monthly_revenue_protected,
  ROUND(COUNT(*) * 0.10 * AVG(monthly_charges) * 12, 2) AS annual_revenue_protected
FROM customer_churn_clean
WHERE contract = 'Month-to-month'
  AND churn = 'Yes';
